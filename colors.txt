Colors:

Background: #121212 (very dark gray/black)
Surface / Cards: #1E1E1E (slightly lighter dark gray)
Borders / Dividers: #2C2C2C

Text Colors:

Primary Text: #E0E0E0 (off-white, easy on eyes)
Secondary Text / Subtle: #A0A0A0 (gray)
Accent Text / Highlights: #FFD369 (warm yellow)

Accent / Buttons:

Primary Button / Accent: #4CAF50 (green)
Secondary / Info: #2196F3 (blue)

Example: #4CAF50 → hover #66BB6A
